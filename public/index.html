Why Secure?

Our secure chat ensures your messages are never stored. It's a one-time chat where, once closed, the conversation does not exist anymore. Your privacy is important.

How It Works?

There are two ways to use our secure chat app:

Creating a Room:

  1. Think of a unique room ID.
  2. Decide upon a display name.
  3. Join and share the details with your friends to chat in the same room.
  4. Password and room size are optional.

Joining a Room:

  1. Enter the room ID.
  2. Provide your name.
  3. If a password exists, enter it to join the room.

Key Features

  1. End-to-End encrypted
  2. Anonymous chat
  3. No message history
  4. Share location
