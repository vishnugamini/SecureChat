Why Secure?

Our secure chat ensures your messages are never stored. It's a one-time chat where, once closed, the conversation does not exist anymore. Your privacy is important.

How It Works?

To use our secure chat, follow these steps:

  1. Decide upon a display name.
  2. Think of a unique room ID.
  3. Enter the room name and share it with your friends to chat in the same room.

Key Features

  1. Anonymous chat
  2. No message history
  3. Share location
